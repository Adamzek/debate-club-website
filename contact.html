Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How can I join the Debate Club? Simply fill out the registration form linked above, and our team will reach out to you.
  • Do I need prior experience in debating to join? No, we welcome everyone! Attend beginner sessions on Mondays and open sessions on other days.
  • Can I participate in competitions right away? New members are encouraged to attend training sessions first. After some practice, you can participate.
  • How do I know about upcoming training sessions? Updates will be shared regularly in our WhatsApp group.
