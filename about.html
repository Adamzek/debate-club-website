Who We Are

  • The IIUM English Debate Club (EDC) is a community of passionate debaters and critical thinkers from diverse backgrounds. Our mission is to develop eloquent speakers who can present their ideas with clarity and confidence, contributing to a well-informed and open-minded student body.

  • At IIUM EDC, we believe in fostering a spirit of collaboration, respect, and intellectual curiosity. Through friendly competitions, regular practice sessions, and constructive feedback, we encourage our members to refine their skills and build strong connections with fellow students.

Our Story

Founding of IIUM English Debate Club

1996

The club was founded to provide a platform for students to develop critical thinking & public speaking skills, fostering a community of professional individuals.

First Regional Championship Win

2005

The club won its first regional debate competition, marking a milestone in establishing its reputation within Malaysia's debating community.

Top 10 Finish at the World Debate Championships

2010

The club gained international attention by competing in global debate tournaments, solidifying its position as one of Malaysia’s top debate teams.

IIUM Hosts Our First National Debate Series

2017

The club successfully hosted IIUM’s first MNUDC in 2017, bringing together top debate teams from across the country to showcase their skills.

Becoming the Premier Debate Club

2023

The club solidifies its reputation as one of Malaysia's top debate teams, securing multiple national competitions & representing the country on the global stage.
