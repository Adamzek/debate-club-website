Welcome to the Official IIUM English Debate Club Website!

Q: What is the IIUM English Debate Club (EDC)?
A: The IIUM EDC is a vibrant community within IIUM, dedicated to fostering public speaking and critical thinking skills through English-language debating.

Q: What makes EDC unique?
A: EDC stands out for its commitment to nurturing persuasive and analytical thinkers. Members are encouraged to challenge ideas, think critically, and communicate effectively.

Q: What achievements does the club have?
A: The EDC actively competes in national and international tournaments, earning numerous accolades and providing members with invaluable competitive experience.

Q: How can students benefit from joining EDC?
A: By joining EDC, students enhance their debating skills, boost their confidence, and gain exposure to diverse perspectives in a supportive environment.
